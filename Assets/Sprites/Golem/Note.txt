Golem Sprite Dimensions are 112x128 pixels
each one animated at a consistent 100ms per frame (10fps)
Intro animation is 64 total frames.
Jump animation is 9 total frames.
Land animation is 12 total frames.
Runcycle animation is 16 total frames and loops.
Sprintcycle animation is 20 total frames and loops.
Walkcycle animation is 22 total frames and loops.  


-The pedestal sprite is at the start of the intro animation.

-the Standing sprite is at the end of the intro animation, end of the land animation, and the start of the jump animation. (it's also a frame in the walkcycle)

-the midair sprite is the end of the jump animation and start of the land animation.
